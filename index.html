Arizona Sunsets

Arizona Sunsets are tend to be orange, purple, pink, and blue. The variation in colors is due to the high-level storm systems with clouds at 20,000 feet causing bright colors like orange to rainbowlike colors to be seen through the atmosphere.

Arizona's flag

The flag of Arizona consists of 13 rays of red and weld-yellow on the top half. The red and yellow symbolize the Spanish conquistadores that explored this part of America; this is because the flag is inspired in the current flag of Spain.
